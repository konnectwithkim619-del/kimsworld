Happily Ever After... Don't Bet On It

Dr. Kim Steffes spent a lifetime caring for everyone else—as a pediatric emergency room physician, wife, mother, and the dependable person others could always count on. But when disability ended her medical career and medication triggered devastating compulsive behaviors, she lost nearly everything she had spent a lifetime building. Structured around the songs that marked life's defining moments, Happily Ever After... Don't Bet On It is an honest memoir of addiction, redemption, forgiveness, and the long journey home. More than a story about gambling, it is a story about identity, resilience, and discovering that hope can emerge from even life's greatest losses. This is a reminder that our worst chapter never has to be our final one.
